About DecodBiz

India's first Shark Tank powered startup recommendation engine. Free, data driven and built to help every aspiring entrepreneur find the right business for their profile.

Our Story

Why We Built DecodBiz

Every year, millions of Indians want to start their own business but don't know where to begin. They watch Shark Tank India and see inspiring entrepreneurs but cannot connect those ideas to their own situation.

DecodBiz bridges that gap. We processed **751 Shark Tank India startups** and **1,485 global startups** to build a matching engine that connects your profile to the right business idea.

We also map each startup to government funding schemes so you know exactly how to fund your dream business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DecodBiz free to use?

Yes. DecodBiz is completely free and requires no login.

Is DecodBiz affiliated with Shark Tank India?

No. DecodBiz is an independent platform built using publicly available data.

How does BizMatch work?

BizMatch uses a scoring algorithm based on investment, domain, risk level, city tier and work preference.

Are government scheme links official?

Yes. All links redirect to official government portals.
