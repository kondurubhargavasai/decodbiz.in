About DecodBiz

DecodBiz is India's first **data-driven startup discovery platform** that helps aspiring entrepreneurs find the right business idea based on their investment, skills, and risk profile.

The Problem

Starting a Business is Hard

Millions of Indians dream about starting a business, but most people don't know **which business idea is right for them**.

They watch inspiring shows like Shark Tank India but struggle to connect those ideas to their own budget, city, and experience level.

As a result, many entrepreneurs either delay their journey or choose businesses without proper research.

Our Solution

Introducing BizMatch

DecodBiz solves this problem with **BizMatch** — a smart recommendation engine that connects your profile with real startup ideas.

Simply enter your: • Investment range • Risk tolerance • Business domain interest • City tier • Work style

And BizMatch instantly recommends startups that match your profile.
